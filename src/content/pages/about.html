About Simon Painting Co.

At Simon Painting Co., we believe your home or business deserves the same care and attention as our own. Founded during the challenges of the Covid era, this business was born from a deep passion for craftsmanship and customer satisfaction. With over 10 years of experience in the construction industry, I set out to raise the standard for quality and service in the painting and home improvement world.

Our Promise

I love what I do, and I take pride in the opportunity to create and improve spaces every day. Whether it’s a fresh coat of paint, a new floor, or a complete custom renovation, we approach every project with integrity, craftsmanship, and a genuine dedication to your satisfaction.

We’re also proud to offer military discounts as a small way of giving back to those who serve.

Let’s bring your vision to life—with skill, passion, and precision.
